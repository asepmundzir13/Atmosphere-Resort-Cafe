Atmosphere

LOCATED IN THE HEART OF BANDUNG, ATMOSPHERE RESORT CAFÉ OFFERS A WIDE RANGE OF THE FINEST CUISINES, IN A CASUAL AND STYLISH ATMOSPHERE. THE INTERIOR DESIGN IS INSPIRED FROM TRADITIONAL BALINESE ARCHITECTURES AND EXTENDS WITH COZY AMBIENCE OF POLISHED WOODS, GAZEBOS AND VARIOUS PLANTS AROUND THE PONDS, WHICH GIVES BEAUTIFUL VIEW AND NATURAL IMPRESSION.
